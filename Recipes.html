The BEST NACHOS O.A.T. - 4/22/25

Best nachos OF ALL TIME that is...

This is a recipe passed down to me from my Uncle... Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag of tortilla chips
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 1 cup of shredded cheese
  • 1 can of queso dip
  • 1/2 cup of diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of diced onions

Toppings:

  • Guacamole
  • Sour cream
  • Pickled jalapenos

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350째F (175째C).
  2. Spread the tortilla chips on a baking sheet.
  3. Top with black beans, cheese, onions, tomatoes, and queso dip.
  4. Bake for 10-15 minutes until cheese is melted.
  5. Top with guacamole, sour cream, and jalapenos.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Valentines Heart Pastries - 4/19/25

Hello Everyone! I made these pastries last valentines day with my partner and they were delish!

Try this one out with a loved one... <3

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cream cheese
  • Sliced strawberries
  • Sanding sugar (for sprinkling)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400째F (200째C).
  2. Roll out the puff pastry and cut into heart shapes.
  3. Mix cream cheese with sugar and spread on the hearts.
  4. Add sliced strawberries on top of the cream cheese.
  5. Top with heart shaped edges.
  6. Brush with egg wash and sprinkle with sanding sugar.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.
